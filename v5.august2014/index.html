{Frontend Web Development & Design}

I am a sophomore year Frontend Web Development & Design student at The Art Institute of Portland. I love what I do. It's my passion, my soul, my love. My life blood.

I am a process oriented Frontend Web Developer & Designer with a passion for excellence and high quality design and development, with a primary focus in Frontend Web Development. I tend toward a strong use of the grid and minimalist, large imagery in my web design style. However, I enjoy working with any client's needs and finding the appropriate solution for the client and users' needs.

My Git Hub account is my most current and up-to-date work in web development. It is updated with new personal projects and work I have done in my classes almost daily.

In my off time I stay grounded, or in the flow as it were, with whitewater kayaking and alpine skiing. Time outdoors is a life balancer and brings in new ideas, fresh perspectives and new energy to then bring to my work and life.
